Analysis

For each of the top users, I am going to analyse their top Tweets from the protest. I hope to look at the language, timing, and potential effect of at their most popular Tweet created during the protest. I will use this information to explain the role of these popular users in mobalizing the protest and further to highlight the power that social media plays in popular protest.

Basically, the visualizations help support the idea that information during this protest was spread by influenchial users. Their Tweets were immensly popular and spread virally.
